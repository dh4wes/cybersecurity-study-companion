Backup Strategy means planned approach for copying and retaining data so it can be restored after loss or corruption. Its practical job is that it protects business continuity by ensuring important data can be recovered after failure, deletion, or attack. Mechanically, it chooses what to back up, where to store copies, and how often to test restoration so recovery remains practical.
