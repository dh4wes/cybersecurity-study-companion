Threat means potential source of harm that could exploit a weakness or disrupt normal operations. Its practical job is that it explains who or what might cause damage, theft, misuse, or outage. Mechanically, it becomes actionable when a capable actor or condition meets an exposed weakness and a path to the target. Vulnerability means weakness in design, implementation, configuration, or process that can be exploited. Its practical job is that it creates an opportunity for a threat to bypass protection or cause harm. Mechanically, it appears when a flaw or misconfiguration leaves a reachable gap that an attacker or failure condition can abuse.
