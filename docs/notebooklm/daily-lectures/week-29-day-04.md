## 4. Key distinctions and common confusions
- Vendor Risk vs Risk Owner — Vendor Risk: Risk introduced by third-party suppliers and service providers. Risk Owner: Person or role accountable for deciding how a specific risk is handled.
- Purpose vs mechanism — be able to separate what Vendor Risk is for from how it actually performs that job.
- Recognition vs diagnosis — knowing the term name is not enough; know what evidence would make you pick it in a scenario.
