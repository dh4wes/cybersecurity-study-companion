Critical Infrastructure means systems essential to societal and economic stability. Its practical job is that it includes essential services whose disruption would significantly affect safety, economy, health, or national security. Mechanically, it depends on interlocking services, suppliers, and control systems, so failure in one area can cascade into others. Business Impact Analysis means process for identifying operational effects of disruption over time. Its practical job is that it estimates process disruption effects, recovery priorities, and tolerable downtime for critical business functions. Mechanically, it maps business processes to dependencies, outage tolerances, and recovery needs so disruption consequences can be measured.
